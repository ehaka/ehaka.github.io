Biography

I am a mathematics PhD student at the University of Jyväskylä under the supervision of Associate Professor Enrico Le Donne since April 2015. My thesis work is to study the regularity of geodesics and the Sard problem in sub-Riemannian geometry, with a particular focus on the case of sub-Riemannian Carnot groups.
