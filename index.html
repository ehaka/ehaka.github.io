Biography

I am a postdoc researcher at SISSA. I completed my PhD in August 2019 at the University of Jyväskylä under the supervision of Enrico Le Donne. My work mainly revolves around the regularity of geodesics and the Sard problem in sub-Riemannian geometry, with a particular focus on the case of sub-Riemannian and sub-Finsler Carnot groups.
