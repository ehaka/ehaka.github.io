In this section, we will use our complete list of gradings to determine a list of Heintze groups over a given nilpotent Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}\text{.}\) A Heintze group is a simply connected Lie group over \(\mathbb{R}\) whose Lie algebra is a semidirect product of a nilpotent Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}\) and \(\mathbb{R}\) via a derivation \(\alpha \in \der(\mathfrak{g})\) so that the roots of its characteristic polynomial have strictly positive real parts.
