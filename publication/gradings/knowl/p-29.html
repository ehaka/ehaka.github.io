Suppose the indexing set \(S\) of a grading \(\mathcal V\) can be embedded into a group \(A\) such that \([V_\alpha,V_\beta]\subset V_{\alpha\star\beta}\) for all \(\alpha,\beta\in A\text{,}\) where we define \(V_\alpha=0\) for \(\alpha\notin S\text{.}\) Then the resulting \(A\)-grading is called a realization of the grading \(\mathcal V\text{.}\)
