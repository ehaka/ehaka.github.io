Finally, the weights of Rumin 3-forms in \(E_0^3\) in terms of \(\mathbf{a}=(a_1,a_2,a_3)\) are

\begin{align*} w(\theta_{2,5,6}+\theta_{1,2,3})_{(a_1,a_2,a_3)}\amp=2a_1+2a_2;\\ w(\theta_{2,3,5})_{(a_1,a_2,a_3)}\amp=a_1+2a_2+a_3;\\ w(\theta_{2,3,6})_{(a_1,a_2,a_3)}\amp=3a_1+3a_2-a_3;\\ w(\theta_{1,3,4}-\theta_{4,5,6})_{(a_1,a_2,a_3)}\amp=4a_1+2a_2;\\ w(\theta_{1,4,5})_{(a_1,a_2,a_3)}\amp=3a_1+a_2+a_3;\\ w(\theta_{1,4,6})_{(a_1,a_2,a_3)}\amp=5a_1+2a_2-a_3;\text{.} \end{align*}

In order to find

\begin{gather*} \max_{(a_1,a_2,a_3)\in\mathbb{R}_+^3}\bigg\lbrace\frac{\delta N_{\min}(3)}{Q_{(a_1,a_2,a_3)}}\bigg\rbrace \end{gather*}

we once again re-express it as a linear optimization problem and obtain the result \(\frac{1}{10}\) by taking \(a_1=a_2=a_3=1\text{,}\) so that \(\ell^{q,p}H^3(G)\neq 0\) for \(\frac{1}{p}-\frac{1}{q}\lt\frac{1}{10}\text{.}\)
