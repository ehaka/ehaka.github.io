The case \(k=2\) follows by anticommutativity of the Lie bracket. Namely, if \([V_{\alpha_1},V_{\alpha_2}]\neq 0\) then we find that

\begin{equation*} 0\neq [V_{\alpha_1},V_{\alpha_2}] = -[V_{\alpha_2},V_{\alpha_1}] \subset V_{\alpha_1\star\alpha_2}\cap V_{\alpha_2\star\alpha_1}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Since the layers of the grading are distinct, this shows that \(\alpha_1\star\alpha_2=\alpha_2\star\alpha_1\text{.}\)
