Theorem 4.18.

Let \(G\) be a Carnot group of dimension \(n\) and homogeneous dimension \(Q\text{.}\) If

\begin{equation*} 1\le p,q\le\infty\textit{ and } \frac{1}{p}-\frac{1}{q}\lt \frac{\delta N_{\min}(h)}{Q} \end{equation*}

then the \(L^{q,p}\) cohomology of \(G\) in degree \(h\) does not vanish.
