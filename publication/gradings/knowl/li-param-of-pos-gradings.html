Item i.
Consider the maximal grading \(\mathcal{V}\) of \(\mathfrak{g}\) over \(\mathbb{Z}^k\text{.}\) Up to automorphism, positive gradings of \(\g\) are in one-to-one correspondence with the projections from \(\mathbb{Z}^k\) to \(\mathbb{R}\) mapping the weights of \(\mathcal{V}\) to strictly positive numbers. A parametrisation of these projections gives a parametrisation of positive gradings.
