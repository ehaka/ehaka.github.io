An \(\RR\)-grading \(\mathcal{V} \colon \mathfrak{g} = \bigoplus_{\alpha \in \RR} V_\alpha \) is positive if \(\alpha\gt 0\) for all the weights of \(\mathcal{V}\text{.}\) If such a grading exists for \(\mathfrak{g}\text{,}\) then \(\mathfrak{g}\) is said to be positively gradable. Also for any particular grading, one may ask if it can be realized as a positive grading.
