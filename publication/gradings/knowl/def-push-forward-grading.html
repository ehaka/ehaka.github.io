Definition 2.6.

Let \(\mathcal V : \mathfrak g = \bigoplus_{\alpha \in A}V_\alpha \) be an \(A\)-grading for some group \(A\text{.}\) Given an automorphism \(\Phi \in \mathrm{Aut}(\mathfrak g )\text{,}\) a group \(B \) and a homomorphism \(f \colon A \to B \text{,}\) we define the push-forward grading \(f_* \Phi(\mathcal V) : \mathfrak g = \bigoplus_{\beta \in B}W_\beta \) over \(B\text{,}\) where

\begin{equation*} W_\beta= \bigoplus_{\alpha \in f^{-1}(\beta)} \Phi(V_{\alpha}). \end{equation*}

When \(\Phi=\pp{Id}\text{,}\) we simply denote \(f_* \pp{Id} (\mathcal{V}) = f_* \mathcal{V}\text{.}\)
