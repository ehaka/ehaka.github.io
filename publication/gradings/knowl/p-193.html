Given a positive grading \(\mathcal V : \g = \bigoplus_{\alpha \in \RR_+} V_\alpha\text{,}\) we call the quantity

\begin{equation*} Q = \sum_{\alpha \in \RR_+}\alpha \dim V_\alpha \end{equation*}

the homogeneous dimension of \(\mathcal V\text{.}\) If \(\g\) is stratifiable, the homogeneous dimension is understood with respect to the stratification. We also define for each \(h=1,\ldots,n\) the number

\begin{gather*} \delta N_{\min}(h)= \min_{\theta\in E_0^h} w(\theta)-\max_{\tilde{\theta}\in E_0^{h-1}}w(\tilde{\theta})\,. \end{gather*}
